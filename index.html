A personal collection of apps

LightSuite

A collection of personal wellness apps and tools built for real moments in real lives, designed to help people understand their own patterns and advocate for themselves.

All screens shown with sample data only  ·  no personal information is displayed

The apps

01

Spotlight

Know your patterns. Walk in informed.

A cycle-tracking app built around the idea that the people who know their bodies best are the ones who show up to appointments with data. Spotlight logs symptoms, moods, and patterns over time, giving users something concrete to point to in conversations with their care team.

Cycle tracking Pattern analysis iOS Web

Currently in beta testing

02

Baselight

Evidence that you've been here before.

Built for people who go through difficult periods and sometimes lose sight of how much they've already overcome. Baselight surfaces a record of resilience, a quiet, private archive that says: you've been here before, and you got through it.

Resilience tracking Mood logging iOS

Currently in beta testing

03

Nightlight

Rest here for a moment. You are safe.

A dream journal with AI-powered reflection. Log dreams, rate vividness, heaviness, and echo, and receive grounded, science-based reflections on what your nervous system may have been processing. Pattern analysis surfaces trends over time. A personal sanctuary card holds your own words and images.

Dream journaling AI reflection Pattern analysis iOS

Currently in development

About the builder

All three apps in the Light Suite were built in SwiftUI and are currently in beta. None are on the App Store, yet.
